The No Pet Left Behind Project

What It Is

We believe that pets, especially those raised in homes, shouldn't be left on the streets. The No Pet Left Behind Project is our way of reducing that. We offer a free service where people can drop off their pets, which prevents them from being abandoned on the streets. We put the pets in shelters, where they can be spayed/neutered, taken care of, then adopted out.

The Impact

Here are some stats:
• Around 3.9 million dogs are abandoned or given up to shelters each year.
• 3.2 million cats find themselves in a shelter every year - either born in a shelter, surrendered or abandoned.
