About Us!

Our Mission

Our mission is to connect as many animals friends with as many loving families as possible.
We partner with community partners, animal shelters, and people like you, to help pets find their forever homes.

Our Founding

Fur-Ever Friends was created and designed by Tessa Heimberger so that all people would have a chance to choose a pet for their family, and that all animals would have a chance to live in a loving home. "I love my cat Olivia, she is one of my best friends." says Tessa, "And I think all pets deserve loving homes."

Our Partners

We partner with community shelters and pounds to connect pets with people. They give us information and pictures about a pet, and we post it on our website. We have worked with over 50 different shelters to help pets.
We also partner with other businesses, especially small businesses. They help us host in-person adoption events and pet food and supply drives. Many shelters are low on such items, so we help them out when we can.
