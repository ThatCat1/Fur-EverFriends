About Us!

Our Mission

Our mission is to connect as many animals friends with as many loving families as possible.
We partner with community partners, animal shelters, and people like you, to help pets find their forever homes.

Contact Us

Feel free to reach out at any time and contact us.




Our Founding

Fur-Ever Friends was created and designed by Tessa Heimberger so that all people would have a chance to choose a pet for their family, and that all animals would have a chance to live in a loving home. "I love my cat Olivia, she is one of my best friends." says Tessa, "And I think all pets deserve loving homes."

Our Partners

We partner with community shelters and pounds to connect pets with people. They give us information and pictures about a pet, and we post it on our website. We have worked with over 50 different shelters to help pets.
We also partner with other businesses, especially small businesses. They help us host in-person adoption events and pet food and supply drives. Many shelters are low on such items, so we help them out when we can.
Sign up for our newsletter, contact us, or follow us on social media to learn about our adoption events and our pet supply drives.

Hiring, Internships and Volunteering

Our shelter partners are often short on help, so we help out. We work wiht shelters around the state to provide job opportunities for pet caretakers, internship opportunities fo people who want to go into veterinarian proffessions, or just want to learn more about pets, and volunteer opportunites for those who just want to help animals.

Jobs: Are you interested in working at a pet shelter? Apply under our jobs tab! We refer you to shelters in your area, where they can hire you directly.

Internships: Are you in a veterinarian program, going to school to work with any types of animals, or are a pet crae specialist? Apply to our intern program! We work with you to provide in-the-field experience with animals and animal care.

Volunteering: We love volunteers! They are the largest part of our (metaphorical) team. You can volunteer at any age, with any experience.
