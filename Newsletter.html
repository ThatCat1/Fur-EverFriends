Our Newsletter

We have two types of emails: Fur-Ever Friends, and the Pet Post.
Fur-Ever Friends emails are about the organization, and our events; pet adoption events, pet supply drives, and any other important information you need to know about us.
• These emails tend to go out once every 3 weeks
Pet Post Emails are about the things we find interesting, and think you might as well as a pet owner and/or animal lover. These emails usually contain a few pices of information or articles, and usually have something for everyone.
• These emails tend to go out once every 1.5 weeks
We are committed to not clogging up your email with spam and junk you don't need, but giving you something that is relevant to you.
